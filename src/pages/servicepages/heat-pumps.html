Heat Pump FAQs

Learn more about heat pump systems and how they can benefit your home.

  • A heat pump extracts heat from the outside air and transfers it into your home. Modern cold-climate heat pumps can operate efficiently even in Michigan's cold temperatures.

  • Yes! A heat pump functions exactly like an air conditioner in the summer, but it also provides heat in the winter, making it a complete year-round comfort solution.

  • A dual-fuel system combines an electric heat pump with a gas furnace. The system automatically switches between them to provide the most efficient heating based on the outside temperature.
