About True Heating and Cooling

True Heating and Cooling is a team of dedicated Michigan Mechanical contractors (classes 1, 2, 5, and 7) committed to providing top-tier HVAC services. With a focus on energy efficiency and customer satisfaction, we bring expert solutions to every home and business across Michigan.

We are proud to partner with DTE and Consumer Energy to provide our clients with the best rebates and energy-saving programs. Our technicians are NATE certified and EPA 608 Universal certified, ensuring that every job is handled with the highest level of professionalism and expertise.
